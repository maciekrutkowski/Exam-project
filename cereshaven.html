Cereshaven

Cereshaven is a small park located near the Ceresbyen residential area. Once a beer brewery, it is now open to public as a recreational spot not only for the inhabitants of Ceresbyen.

Based in a good location with not only plenty of bus options to take but also within a 15 minutes walking distance from the Aarhus Cathedral on Store Torv, it is easy to get there.

There are places to relax and enjoy good weather as well as spend some time near the Aarhus river.
Ceresbyen, 8000 Aarhus C

Opening hours: Opened all day

Bus line: 12, 4A, 3A
